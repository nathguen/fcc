Building Social Connections

We at, the Foundation for Connecting Communities, seek to build social connections with the community through:

  1. Family-to-Family (F2F) Mentorship
  2. School-Age Kids Connections
  3. Civic & Citizenship Education
  4. Social Events, Recreation, and Entertainment

Our Mentors

  1. Provide assistance to their mentee families as time and resources allow
  2. Get satisfaction from serving others and seeing families being successful
  3. Conduct needs assessment and connect the mentee to community re-sources to meet the identified needs
  4. Make a positive difference in the lives of connecting families
  5. Assist family in learning English and prepare them for citizenship test
  6. Facilitate new families in coping with laws and US societal norms
  7. FCC encourages mentors to teach the connecting families to become self-reliant. Hence, mentors makes an effort to help mentees gain ability & confidence to serve themselves
  8. FCC developed tools such as FCC App to facilitate your involvement
