Economic Connections

Building Economic Connections

We at, the Foundation for Connecting Communities, seek to build economic connections with the community through:

  1. Employment
  2. Education Opportunities
  3. Financial Literacy
  4. Home Ownership

Employment Assistance

We assist businesses in hiring individuals with specific skills among connecting communities.

We partner with businesses and/or government institutions to facilitate employment accessibility and improve work standards and safety in the workplace.

We assist connecting individuals with filling out job applications and professional advising.

Education Opportunities

We provide career/academic plan coaching (especially for the out-of-school-system individuals), connect them to educational institutions such as job corps, colleges, and universities. FCC also incentivizes excellent performance in both academic and extra-curriculum activities.

Financial Literacy

We provide educational workshop on financial literacy, banking system, credit worthiness tips, and saving habits aiming at economic self-sufficiency. We also provide individual-based technical assistance, and assistance with family-based economic planning.

Home Ownership

We connect our clients to real estate management businesses and facilitate rental and/or purchase contract processes.
